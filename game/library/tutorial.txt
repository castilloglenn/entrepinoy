TITLE : HOW TO PLAY ENTREPINOY
PARAGRAPH  :  ENTREPINOY IS AN IDLE BUSINESS SIMULATION GAME THAT ALLOWS PLAYERS TO EXPERIENCE HANDLING AND MANAGING BUSINESSES WITHOUT LOSING ANYTHING IN REAL LIFE.
TITLE : I. BUSINESS
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN HAVE MANY BUSINESSES AND MANAGE THEM TO EARN MONEY.
SUB-TITLE : PURCHASE BUSINESS
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN OWN THIS BUSINESS.
SUB-TITLE : START BUSINESS
PARAGRAPH : BEGIN YOUR BUSINESS OPERATION AT ANY TIME OF DAY FOR AN 8-HOUR PERIOD.
SUB-TITLE : HIRE EMPLOYEE
PARAGRAPH : EMPLOYEES WILL HANDLE YOUR BUSINESS AUTOMATICALLY FOR EIGHT HOURS, ALLOWING YOU TO FOCUS ON OTHER MATTERS.
SUB-TITLE : SELL BUSINESS 
PARAGRAPH : IT WILL SELL YOUR BUSINESS.
SUB-TITLE : UPGRADE
PARAGRAPH : IT CAN INCREASE INCOME AND OPERATION COSTS.
SUB-TITLE : CUSTOMERS
PARAGRAPH : CUSTOMERS WILL GO TO YOUR BUSINESSES TO PURCHASE SOMETHING WHICH WILL GENERATE YOUR INCOME.
TITLE : II. REGION MAP
SUB-TITLE : YOU CAN MANAGE YOUR BUSINESSES FROM LOCATION A TO F BY TRAVELING TO OTHER LOCATIONS.
TITLE : III. MISSIONS
SUB-TITLE : YOU CAN EARN MONEY BY COMPLETING A MISSION.
PARAGRAPH : EVERY DAY, THERE ARE THREE DIFFERENT MISSIONS.
PARAGRAPH : MISSIONS WILL BE RESET THE FOLLOWING DAY.
TITLE : IV. ACHIEVEMENTS
PARAGRAPH : THIS IS YOUR OVERALL RECORD OF YOUR GAMEPLAY IN THE GAME.
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN SEE YOUR ACHIEVEMENTS AND HOW FAR YOU'VE PROGRESSED IN THE GAME.
TITLE : V. PART-TIME
SUB-TITLE : YOU WILL WORK AS A DATA ENCODER AND YOU WILL BE REWARDED WITH AN AMOUNT OF MONEY BASED ON YOUR PERFORMANCE.
PARAGRAPH : THIS PART-TIME JOB CAN BE DONE ONCE A DAY.
PARAGRAPH : TO MOVE ON TO THE NEXT SET OF WORDS, YOU MUST TYPE THE CORRECT SPELLING OF A WORD.
PARAGRAPH : AT THE BOTTOM OF THE SCREEN, YOU WILL SEE THE PROGRESS, SUCH AS ACCURACY, WORDS PER MINUTE, AND SO ON.
TITLE : VI. NEWS
SUB-TITLE : THE DAILY NEWS CAN ALSO SERVE AS YOUR CALENDAR AND MAP, AND YOU CAN SEE THE DAY, TIME, AND YOUR LOCATION ON IT.
PARAGRAPH : IT HELPES YOU DETERMINE HOW MANY CUSTOMERS ARE ON THE STREET, FOR EXAMPLE, A RUSH HOUR OR A LOW CUSTOMERS VOLUME.
PARAGRAPH : IT ALSO LETS YOU KNOW IF YOU HAVE NOT DONE THE PART-TIME JOB AND DEPOSITED MONEY.
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN DETERMINE WHETHER THE STOCK MARKET OR CRYPTOCURRENCIES ARE UNSTABLE OR IF NOW IS A GOOD TIME TO INVEST.
TITLE : VII. BANK
PARAGRAPH : THE BANK WILL PROVIDE YOU WITH MONEY EXCHANGE AND BORROWING SERVICES.
SUB-TITLE : DEPOSIT 
PARAGRAPH : ENABLES YOU TO KEEP MONEY.
PARAGRAPH : EVERY MONTH, STORED MONEY GROWS BY 5%.
SUB-TITLE : WITHDRAW
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN RECEIVE ALL OF YOUR MONEY BACK.
SUB-TITLE : LOAN 
PARAGRAPH : IT ALLOWS YOU TO BORROW MONEY.
PARAGRAPH : TO GET A LOAN FROM THE BANK, YOU MUST OWN AT LEAST ONE BUSINESS.
PARAGRAPH : YOU MUST PAY BORROWED MONEY FOR 3 MONTHS.
PARAGRAPH : AS A COLLATERAL, YOU MUST HAVE THE HIGHEST LEVEL OF BUSINESS OWNERSHIP.
TITLE : VIII. DIGITAL ASSETS
PARAGRAPH : THESE DAYS MANY ENTREPRENEURS AND BUSINESSMEN WANT TO TRY THE INVESTING STOCK MARKET AND CRYPTOCURRENCIES SO WE WILL GIVE YOU A GUIDE.
SUB-TITLE : CRYPTO
PARAGRAPH : CRYPTOCURRENCIES, ALSO KNOWN AS VIRTUAL CURRENCIES, ARE ONE OF THE MOST RECENT FORMS OF MONEY.
PARAGRAPH : THE CRYPTOCURRENCIES WILL CHANGE THEIR VALUE WITHIN SECONDS SO BE CAREFUL WHEN BUYING AND SELLING CRYPTOCURRENCIES BECAUSE YOU WILL LOSE A LOT OF MONEY THAN GAINING A PROFIT.
PARAGRAPH : YOU WILL ALSO  LEARN ABOUT BUYING AND SELLING FOR CRYPTOCURRENCIES.
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN BUY AND SELL THE CRYPTOCURRENCIES WITHIN THE DAY AND IT WILL CHANGE THE NEXT DAY AT 8 AM.
SUB-TITLE : STOCKS
PARAGRAPH : THERE ARE A LOT OF CRYPTOCURRENCIES LIKE BITCOIN, ETHEREUM, DOGECOIN, AND SO ON. 
PARAGRAPH : THE TERM STOCK MARKET REFERS TO SEVERAL EXCHANGES IN WHICH SHARES OF PUBLICLY HELD COMPANIES ARE BOUGHT AND SOLD.
PARAGRAPH : UNLIKE THE CRYPTOCURRENCY, THE STOCKS WILL CHANGE THEIR VALUE WITHIN THE DAY SO BE CAREFUL ALSO FOR BUYING AND SELLING THE STOCKS SO YOU WILL NOT LOSE A LOT OF MONEY THAN GAINING A PROFIT.
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN BUY AND SELL THE STOCKS WITHIN THE DAY AND IT WILL CHANGE IN THE NEXT DAY AT 8 AM.
TITLE : IX. SETTINGS
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN MAKE THE SCREEN FULL SCREEN.
PARAGRAPH : YOU CAN LOWER AND HIGHER THE BACKGROUND MUSIC (BGM) AND THE SOUND EFFECTS/ SPECIAL EFFECTS (SFX)
